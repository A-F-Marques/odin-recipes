Steak and Broccoli Srtir Fry

What makes this dish special

Steak and broccoli stir-fry is a classic comfort dish that combines juicy, tender slices of beef with crisp, vibrant broccoli, all tossed together in a savory, garlicky sauce. It's a hearty yet balanced meal, full of flavor and texture, perfect for when you want something both satisfying and wholesome.

Ingredients needed

  • 2 Tablespooon cooking oil
  • 1 Pound ribeye steak
  • Sallt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 honey
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons fresh ginger
  • 1 shallot
  • 1/2 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 2 green onions

Steps

  1. Heat grapeseed oil in a cast iron skillet over medium heat. Season steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Add steak to skillet and cook for 1 to 2 minutes on each side. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Whisk soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, and shallot together in a bowl. Pour sauce into the skillet and bring to a gentle simmer. Add broccoli and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add steak back into the skillet; cook until hot, 1 to 2 minutes. Sprinkle with chopped green onions to serve.
