Lasagna

What makes this dish special

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made of tender pasta sheets layered with rich sauces and melted cheese, baked until golden and bubbling. Hearty and comforting, it's a favorite around the world for both everyday meals and special gatherings.

Ingredients needed

  • One pound of ground meat
  • A diced onion
  • A can of tomato sauce
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two tablespoons of parsley
  • One clove of garlic
  • A dash of sugar
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • lasagna noodles
  • cottage cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layered: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown. Bake for 50 minutes at 190 degrees C.
